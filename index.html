DRAKE

Drake, whose full name is Aubrey Drake Graham, is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, actor, and entrepreneur. He is one of the most prominent and successful figures in the music industry. Drake first gained recognition as an actor on the teen drama series "Degrassi: The Next Generation." However, he is best known for his contributions to the music industry.

KANYE
